About Me

Nov 11, 2024

Values and Personal Philosophy

I aim to serve others through provision and protection.

With this in mind, I explore to uncover challenges. For challenges facilitate growth and growth means I am better able to serve.

In doing all I can for those around me, and accepting that which is out of my control, I am rewarded with peace of mind.

Through this peace, I can presently experience the beauty of life - the absurdity of the infinite complexity that aggregates to form our reality.

Further, the love of those around me plays a crucial role in providing nourishment and invigoration such that I am able and willing to continue in this pursuit.

I hope to, with those around me, engage in a virtuous cycle in which our respective successes serve as inspiration for one another to further grow, and our respective love serves as support for that growth.

Hobbies and Interests

In a primary interest I seek mastery through continued engagement:

  1. Programming & Technology

In secondary interests I seek excellence through frequent engagement:

  1. Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, Philosophy

In tertiary interests I seek casual enjoyment through occasional engagement:

  1. Art, Bouldering, Chess, Hiking, Photography, Skiing, Writing
